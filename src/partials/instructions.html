How to Play

  • 1

    Slide & Rotate Tiles

    Move or spin pieces to align colors, shapes, or numbers.

  • 2

    Complete Objectives

    Clear specific patterns or solve under time/turn limits.

  • 3

    Unlock New Mechanics

    Face shifting boards, blockers, and multi-layered challenges.

  • 4

    Trigger Combos

    Chain reactions give extra points and power moves.

  • 5

    Progress Through Worlds

    Unlock new puzzle environments with unique visual styles and logic patterns.
