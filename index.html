About the Game

Welcome to Puzzle Twist Game, where logic meets motion and every level spins your mind in a new direction!

Slide, rotate, and strategically match tiles to solve increasingly complex puzzles. With rotating mechanics, shifting boards, and unexpected patterns, this isn’t your typical puzzle game — it’s a challenge for thinkers, planners, and quick decision-makers.

Can you master the twist?

  • Designed to challenge your logic and spatial awareness.

  • Rotational mechanics add a new dimension to match-based gameplay.

  • Levels that adapt to your progress with evolving complexity.

How to Play
